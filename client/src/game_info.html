AtlasDash

The World Geography Challenge

01
Overview

Game
Objective

AtlasDash is a fast-paced geography challenge. Your mission is simple — identify as many countries as possible before the timer runs out.

  • 🌍
    Identify the correct country on the world map based on the name shown on screen.
  • ⏱
    Answer as many countries as you can within the 60-second time limit.
  • 🏆
    Maximize your score, climb classroom leaderboards, and prove your geography knowledge.
02
Rules

Game
Rules

Keep these ground rules in mind before you start your dash across the atlas.

  • 🗺
    You must click directly on the country shown on the interactive world map.
  • ❌
    Incorrect clicks deduct points — think before you click!
  • ⏰
    The round ends at 00:00. No additional answers are accepted after time expires.
  • 🔄
    A new country appears automatically after every answer — no skipping allowed.
  • 🚫
    Using external resources or assistance during a classroom session is not permitted.
03
Gameplay

How
to Play

Get started in seconds. Here's your step-by-step guide to playing AtlasDash.

  • 1
    A country name appears on the right panel when the round begins.
  • 2
    Locate and click the correct country on the interactive world map.
  • 3
    Your score updates instantly — green for correct, red for incorrect.
  • 4
    The next country name appears automatically — keep your momentum!
  • 5
    Keep playing until the timer reaches 00:00 and your final score is recorded.

05
Education

Classroom
System

AtlasDash integrates into your classroom experience. Teachers can create sessions, assign rounds, and track student progress in real time.

  • 🏫
    Teachers can create a classroom and share a unique join code with students.
  • 👩‍🎓
    Students join the classroom and compete in teacher-assigned rounds or free-play sessions.
  • 📋
    A live leaderboard displays rankings within the classroom after each round.
  • 📈
    Teachers can view individual progress, scores, and accuracy stats for each student.
